Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries regarding membership, club projects, and operations.

Who can join us? +

Anyone between the ages of 16 to 35, residing in Thane, Maharashtra, who is eager to grow as a leader, contribute to impactful initiatives, build meaningful networks, and become a part of a vibrant and supportive Rotaract family. If you're passionate about service, learning, and making a difference — you're welcome!

What do we do? +

We conduct community service projects, professional development workshops, social events, cultural programs, and international collaborations. It's all about creating impact while having fun and building friendships for life.

How can I join? +

Just reach out to us on Instagram, Facebook, or email — or attend one of our meetings. We'll guide you through the simple membership process and welcome you onboard.
