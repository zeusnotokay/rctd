What is Rotaract & Who are we?

Rotaract is an internationally renowned organization for youth from the age of 18 to 30 years. This youth wing of Rotary International works for the welfare and development of the community through fellowship and personality development.

We at the Rotaract Club of Thane Downtown serve this very purpose since 7th May 2014. We are a family of 75+ Rotaractors at RC Thane Downtown.

"Together We Change Lives, Together We Touchdown. We Are the Rotaractors of THANE DOWNTOWN."
