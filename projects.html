Our Projects

A legacy of sustainable community service, cultural activities, and talent development.

Flagship Initiatives

For over a decade, RCTD has conducted projects aimed at serving the community, promoting fitness, and nurturing professional talents in the Thane region.

RaasLeela

RaasLeela is a flagship cultural celebration designed to bring the Thane community together through the joy of Garba. It serves as one of our biggest public-facing initiatives, promoting RCTD among the youth while celebrating culture and tradition.

Each year, the event witnesses a footfall of 700–1000 enthusiastic participants. The proceeds raised are donated to our Jaipur Foot Donation project, making Raas Leela a perfect blend of celebration and service.

Eloquence

Eloquence is an 8-year-old initiative of the Rotaract Club of Thane Downtown aimed at enhancing public speaking, debate, and essay writing skills among school children.

The project features elocution and extempore competitions, encouraging young participants to express themselves with confidence. Winners are awarded prizes and given opportunities to showcase their talent on larger platforms.

Circle of Love

Circle of Love is a 7-year-old compassionate initiative dedicated to uplifting the transgender community in the Thane region.

Each year, funds raised (over ₹50,000 annually) are used to provide essential monsoon support, including rice, flour, oil, raincoats, sarees, and water purifiers to ensure clean drinking water. COL stands for inclusivity, dignity, and care.

Sports Extravaganza

Sports Extravaganza is aimed at promoting fitness, competition, and teamwork within the Thane community.

We host simultaneous tournaments across 14+ sports over the course of two weekends, open to all age groups. This project instills values of sportsmanship, discipline, and stands as a celebration of energy and unity.

Jaipur Foot Donation (JFD)

For the past 11 years, the Rotaract Club of Thane Downtown has been conducting the Jaipur Foot Donation drive.

Through this initiative, we raise funds to support amputees by providing them with prosthetic legs. The project empowers individuals who have lost mobility due to accidents or disabilities to regain independence.

Rakshapatra

Rakshapatra is a 10-year-old flagship initiative conducted on the occasion of Rakshabandhan, bridging citizens and the armed forces.

We invite school children to write letters to soldiers, thanking them for their service. Every year, we collaborate with 15+ schools, sending over 1,00,000 letters to various army base camps across the country.
